Anurag Nihal

I am a PhD student in Prof. Vedhus Hoskere's group at the University of Houston (Geosensing Systems Engineering & Sciences). I hold a B.S.-M.S. (Dual Degree) from IISER Bhopal in Data Science & Engineering. My research develops deep learning approaches to solve remote sensing applications. I am currently working on damage captioning and assessment using Vision-Language Models for VHR SAR and Optical imagery. My prior work addressed 3D LiDAR semantic segmentation and unsupervised domain adaptation for urban environments.

Research Projects

Jun 2024 - Mar 2025 DST Project

Deep Learning for Emergency Vehicle Accessibility

Investigated emergency vehicle accessibility in Indian urban areas using LiDAR and image data. Developed cross-domain semantic segmentation architectures for 3D point clouds and images. Deployed the pipeline on Nvidia Jetson Nano for real-time edge inference.

LiDAR Semantic Segmentation Edge Deployment Domain Adaptation
Aug 2023 - Apr 2024 Accepted at Neurocomputing

MuSMix: Multi-Source Mixing for UDA-based 3D LiDAR Semantic Segmentation

M.S. Thesis · Guides: Prof. Vaibhav Kumar (IISER Bhopal), Prof. Bharat Lohani (IIT Kanpur)

Proposed a multi-source domain adaptation architecture that uses semantic-aware sample mixing to close domain gaps across 3D LiDAR datasets. Designed loss functions that reduce boundary-point mis-segmentation between classes.

Jan 2023 - Apr 2023

Generation of Soundscape Maps from Indian Street View Images

B.S. Project · Guide: Prof. Vaibhav Kumar, GeoAI4Cities Lab, IISER Bhopal

Curated a multi-annotated dataset of high-resolution 360° Indian street view images with semantic maps and corresponding audio. Proposed two cross-modal conditional GAN architectures for soundscape generation from semantic maps. This work is the first to synthesize audio features from high-resolution semantic maps of Indian street view imagery.

GANs Cross-Modal Soundscapes Street View

Generation of Dense Urban Features using Conditional GANs

Research Internship · Guide: Prof. Vaibhav Kumar, GeoAI4Cities Lab, IISER Bhopal

Extended pix2pix with multiple discriminators and evaluated several generator architectures for synthesizing high-resolution satellite images from semantic labels. Generated synthetic satellite image datasets with pixel-level annotations for downstream segmentation tasks.
